About Us

At STH we pride ourselves in providing evidence-based and patient-centered physiotherapy services of the highest quality in order to help you recover from injuries or surgery, manage chronic conditions, and improve overall physical health. We are committed to working with you to develop a personalised treatment plan that addresses your specific needs. Our team will be with you every step of the way, providing support, guidance, and encouragement as you work towards your goals. Our practice is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and equipment as we are based at the Johan J. Ferreira High Performance Centre, and we have an environment that is designed to make your visit as pleasant and stress-free as possible.
